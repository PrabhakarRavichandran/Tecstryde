FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical website take to build? +

Starter websites take 7–10 days, Professional projects take 14–21 days, and Enterprise projects vary based on scope — typically 3–6 weeks. We always keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you require a deposit upfront? +

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion before the final handoff. This protects both parties.

Do you provide website hosting? +

We don't host websites directly, but we help you set up hosting on platforms like Vercel, Netlify, or your own server for free. We can also recommend reliable hosting providers for your needs.

Can I update the website myself after it's built? +

Absolutely! We can integrate a CMS (like Contentful or Sanity) that allows you to easily update content without any coding knowledge. This can be added to any plan.

What if I need something not listed in the plans? +

We love custom projects! Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a tailored proposal specifically for your needs and budget.
